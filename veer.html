VeeR

User Research

VeeR is a Virtual Reality (VR) app featuring a global VR video content network, providing video contents handpicked from a dynamic community of creators global-wide. VeeR brings true cross-platform experience to customers from mobile (iOS, Android) to Gear VR and Web.

Step By Step

Step One: The Pilot Study
We designed the Pilot Study to predict and evaluate interview time, research cost and potential target interviewee group. Besides, we learned VeeR iOS mobile app throughly to have a better understanding of app special features.

After the Pilot Study, we evaluated our interview time would be around 45 - 60 mins, $20 for each interviewee who completes the interview.

    The app features we needed to pay special attention are :
  • Navigation
  • Normal to VR mode switch
  • User account registration
  • Video play
  • Friend invitation

Step Two: Participants Recuitment
In order to recruiting interviewees, we designed a Participants Recruitment Poster.


Before our study began, we had received lots of people's feedback who were interested in our study and wanted to participate in. After reviewing their background, we decided 5 of them to participate our interview. Because based on psychological study, 5 is the best return on investment by group.

Step Three: In-Depth Interview
For having our interview to be more authentic, we didn't reveal our app info to participants before our interview, so we can record their very first impression and reaction for this app.

We also provided participants to sign a term document to protect their privacy, which informed them we could record and take pictures of them and these materials would be used and only be used for our research study.

During our user research interview, participants were asked to speak out-of-loud for their every action and questions while their doing the best. So we could instantly know their problems and give them some hints if necessary.

    Follow is the tasks we designed to make the interview more pleasant and entertaining.
  • Task 1: Play a random video;
  • Task 2: Switch video from normal mode to VR mode;
  • Task 3: Turn video volume up/down, charge video;
  • Task 4: Find top 25 video from app;
  • Task 5: User account registration
  • Task 6: Enjoy the app.

After finishing all the tasks, we also launched a post-test questionnaire.

    Questions:
  • How do you think about your whole experience? Both pro and con.
  • What kinds of video will you watch to use this app?
  • Will you recommend this app to your friends? Why?
