BloodSugar Diary

iOS APP UX Design

Bloodsugar Dairy is a comprehensive iOS tracking app that offers diabetes patients and the people who have high blood sugar an easy and convenient way to learn and self-manage their health status.

As society developed, the people who suffer high BG become more and more younger-age trend. Once being diagnosed for diabetes, tracking and controlling BG value is ineluctable in their rest daily life.

Thus building an easy, high-efficient and user friendly BG tracking app will offer them a delightful life.

Where we start ...

We read lots of diabetes related published papers, analysis top rated diabetes tracking app from all over the world, and also conducted interviews with an experienced diabetes doctor and a group of target users from different background and age range.

Interview & Analysis

We invited Dr. Wang from West China Hosputal, SCU, who has over 20 yrs professional experience on diabetes treatments, to discuss about diabetes and high BG value from different aspects. We also interviewed a group of people who suffer from diabetes or high BG value. In order to having a better understanding of patients, we chose them from different disease histroy and age range.

    Interview Q & A (partially)
  • - What is the most frequent age range for diabetes diagnosis?
    - 40 ~ 59 is the most frequent diabetes diagnosis age range.
  • - Does the high BG value equal diabetes?
    - Absolutely not. High BG value is only one of symptom for diabetes. It doesn't mean all the high BG value patients are diabetes.
  • - Typical kind of people who might have high BG value/diabetes?
    - People who are in 40 - 59 years old range, and some pregnant woman.
  • - What aspects should be concerned by the high BG patients for maintaining a healthy status?
    - Tracking BG value at least 2 times a day, one before breakfast, the other one after dinner. Control carbs for each meal. Do some daily work out.
